How to Play

In Forged Alliance Forever, you lead a huge army in epic sci-fi battles! Outsmart your opponents by building bases, making tanks, planes, and giant robots, and planning clever attacks. It’s a fast-paced strategy game where thinking meets action!

  • WASD or Arrow Keys: Move your camera around the map.
  • Mouse: Click to select units and give orders like building or attacking.
  • Shift: Hold to queue multiple orders for your units.
  • Ctrl + Number: Assign units to a group for quick control.
  • ESC: Open the in-game menu or pause the game.

Sharpen your skills and rule the battlefield in solo missions or multiplayer wars, only on Forged Alliance Forever!

Description

Forged Alliance Forever is an exciting real-time strategy (RTS) game built on Supreme Commander: Forged Alliance. You command massive armies with tanks, planes, and giant experimental units in a sci-fi world. Whether you’re playing solo campaigns, practicing against AI, or battling real players online, the game tests your strategy and quick thinking. Customize your army, try new maps, and lead your forces to victory!
